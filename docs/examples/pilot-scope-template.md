## Problem To Solve

Describe the one revenue workflow selected for this pilot:

- BidFlow example: missed quote follow-up for roofing repair leads.
- ReputeLoop example: slow or risky Google review replies and dormant customer recovery.

## Deliverables

BidFlow Local:

- Lead intake audit for one workflow.
- Import or manually enter up to 20 pilot leads.
- Score leads and recommend next steps.
- Generate one first delivery pack with estimate draft, proposal draft, and follow-up sequence.
- Weekly owner summary with pipeline value and won/lost notes.

ReputeLoop:

- Review profile audit for one location.
- Import 30-100 reviews by CSV or approved Google Business Profile access.
- Score review risk and draft public-safe replies.
- Generate one first delivery pack with response draft, recovery case, and winback offer where appropriate.
- Weekly owner summary with approved replies, recovery cases, and repeat-booking opportunities.

## Customer Responsibilities

- Pay through live Stripe Checkout.
- Submit lead CSV, review CSV, or setup notes through the private onboarding link.
- Do not submit passwords, API keys, card data, or unrelated sensitive records.
- Review and approve any external messages, public replies, refunds, discounts, or binding quote language before use.
- Confirm that any email/SMS outreach has consent.

## Operator Responsibilities

- Confirm Stripe webhook-created onboarding before sending the private onboarding link.
- Review customer-submitted materials before import.
- Run preview before importing CSV.
- Import only clean CSV submissions.
- Generate and human-QA the first delivery pack.
- Record the outcome and renewal recommendation.

## Success Metric

Choose one primary success metric:

- BidFlow: one won job, one revived quote conversation, or at least 10 hours/month saved.
- ReputeLoop: five approved review replies, one recovered customer conversation, or one repeat booking opportunity.

## Boundaries

- No fake reviews.
- No review gating.
- No incentives for positive reviews.
- No automated SMS/email without consent.
- No public review reply, refund, discount, or admission of liability without manager approval.
- Draft estimates are not binding until the customer approves final pricing and scope.

## Acceptance

- Live payment is confirmed in Stripe.
- Onboarding record exists.
- Customer materials are submitted.
- Operator review is complete.
- Import preview has no row errors.
- First pack is generated and QA-approved.
- Customer outcome is logged.
