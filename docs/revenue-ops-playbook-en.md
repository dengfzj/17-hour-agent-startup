# Revenue Ops Playbook Summary

Recorded on: 2026-06-11

## North Star

The first goal is not broad self-serve scale. It is closing 3-5 paid concierge pilots and proving renewal value with delivery evidence.

## Daily Actions

1. Open the Launch revenue command center.
2. Review open checkout, delivery risk, customer action, and renewal evidence.
3. Prioritize unpaid checkout, delivery deadlines, and customer responses that need follow-up.
4. Record every sales action in the sales activity ledger.
5. Record every customer confirmation or result in the outcome ledger.

## Revenue Evidence Rules

- Only the `RevenuePayment` ledger is collection evidence.
- `SalesSummary.estimatedPipelineValue` is pipeline estimate only.
- `PilotOutcome` is renewal evidence, not collection evidence.
- Recovery link approval is customer action evidence, not collection evidence.

## Sales Rhythm

- New prospect: generate outreach pack.
- Qualified/contacted: log touches.
- Scope sent: create checkout handoff.
- Checkout sent: download order form and follow up on payment.
- Won: move to onboarding and delivery.

## Renewal Rhythm

After first-pack confirmation, record:

- won job
- revived quote
- approved review reply
- recovered customer
- repeat booking
- hours saved

This evidence supports the next renewal or upgrade conversation.
